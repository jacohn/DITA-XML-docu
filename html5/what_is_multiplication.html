What is multiplication

Here you will learn what is division

To successfully perform the task of multiplying 2 numbers, you must know what multiplication is. The term multiplication represents a mathematical operation. multiplication allows you to find a solution that contains the first number .

You use the multiplication (x) button to express the division operation in CohnCalc.

In the multiplication process, you take a number (the first number) and add it to itself a certain number of times (second number).

2 x 3 = 2 + 2 + 2= 6
