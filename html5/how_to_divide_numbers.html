how to divide numbers

Here you will find instructions for the division feature of CohnCalc.

To successfully divide numbers, you must know what is multiplication.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Choose the division (/) button.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Choose the equal (=) button.

The result from the multiplication is displayed.

You want to divide 3 by 2. The equation will look like: 3/2=1.5. You enter 3. Press divide (/). Enter 2. Press equals.

The result 1.5 appears on the screen.
